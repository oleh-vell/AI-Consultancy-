# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Hackathon judges and prospective buyers watching a ~90-second live demo. The
"user" inside the product is an **observer** — a human who watches an autonomous
AI agency work the pipeline, intervening only to fast-forward (hit *Call now*).
The agency's real customers are UK mid-market operators (COOs, ops directors)
whose teams lose time to manual back-office work.

## Product Purpose

AI Consultancy of London is an AI consulting agency that runs itself: it dials
leads, runs a discovery call, drafts a tailored pitch deck from what was said,
and bills the client — with no human in the loop. The product is the dashboard
the human watches: Leads (CRM queue) → live Call → promotion to Account →
Account workspace (Activity, Pitch Deck, Bill). Success = a judge believes the
loop closed itself, end to end, in one sitting.

## Brand Personality

Three words: **autonomous, credible, precise.** The voice of a serious London
consultancy that happens to be a machine — understated confidence, no hype.
Emotionally it should feel like watching something quietly competent do a
day's work in seconds. Perplexity-grade product polish; McKinsey-grade gravity.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI-SaaS purple-on-white with a gradient hero and emoji.
- Chatbot-builder aesthetics; "wizard" stepper UIs.
- Crypto/fintech neon. Loud marketing-landing energy inside the app.
- Anything that reads as a toy or a prototype — this must feel operational.

## Design Principles

- **Show the work, don't narrate it.** The status moving (Queued → Calling →
  Promoted) and the deck appearing *are* the pitch. Let state changes carry it.
- **The machine is calm; one signal means "working."** A single petrol-teal
  accent marks live/agent activity. Everything else stays quiet ink-on-paper.
- **Earned familiarity.** Patterns a Linear/Stripe user already trusts — real
  tables, real tabs, real invoices — so attention goes to the story, not the UI.
- **Every artifact references the call.** Deck and invoice quote what the caller
  actually said; the enrichment must feel causal, not canned.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Body text ≥4.5:1; large/heading text ≥3:1. Full
`prefers-reduced-motion` alternatives for the ring, waveform, typewriter, and
promotion ladder (instant/crossfade fallbacks). Keyboard-operable call composer
and deck navigation; visible focus rings throughout.
